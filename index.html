Who We Are

At Zebrik, a financial technology organization, we seek to create positive economic impact and long-term value for our investors, the companies we invest in, and the communities in which we work. In the market for working capital, we want to change small business lending by making it easier for SMEs to get funding. We do this by using extraordinary people and flexible capital to help companies solve problems. On a global scale, our asset management divisions include private equity, real estate, public debt and equity, growth equity, opportunistic, non-investment grade credit, real assets, and secondary funds.

Tailored To Your Financial Sustainability

Financial planning is complex when your business is the primary source of your wealth. To meet the complex needs of a client, a broad range of customized transparent solutions is provided by Zebrik Venture. We help to create a dynamic road map aligned to your unique priorities and future goals.
